# Axion Workspace🚀

Welcome to **Axion Workspace**—a clean, beautiful, and distraction-free desktop playground designed to map out daily study targets, log deep focus execution metrics, and conquer academic overwhelm. 

Instead of jumping between disconnected note apps, drawing sketchpads, and messy calendar configurations, Axion unifies your entire workflow inside a premium, custom-tailored frosted glass command console that adapts directly to you.

---

## 🎯 What is the Purpose of Axion?

Axion was built out of a real student need: to make the friction of daily tracking disappear. Traditional productivity frameworks often feel like a secondary chore. Axion shifts that dynamic by leveraging personal AI integration and smooth workspace design to turn log tracking into a fluid, visual experience. 

By capturing page numbers, active focus cycles, distraction intervals, and visual scratchpad connection maps, Axion gives you a completely honest, data-driven window into your cognitive velocity and subject mastery over time.

---

## 🛠️ Main Utilities & How to Use It

To get the most out of your Axion Workspace Hub setup, make sure you configure these two foundational pillars:

### 1. 🔑 Bring Your Own AI Engine
Axion doesn't rely on locked external servers—it hooks directly into your own private intelligence layer to run form extractions and conversational assistant prompts.
* Head over to the **Settings** tab on your sidebar.
* Paste your personal **API Key** into the input block.
* Once saved, your natural-language parser activates instantly. You can type or tell the system things like *"Bio 40 mins pages 20 to 35 retention 8"* and watch the forms auto-populate themselves.

### 2. 📅 Live Google Calendar Synchronization
Axion compiles task states, targeted subject revisions, and missed session penalties straight onto your real-world timeline.
* **Important Note:** For security protocols, Google OAuth client access limits login handshakes to explicitly registered developers.
* **Want to activate syncing?** If you want to white-list your personal account or connect your hub to your active Google Calendar timeline, **just DM me directly!** I will add your email coordinates to the Google Cloud Console dashboard backend in under a minute so you can authenticate safely.
